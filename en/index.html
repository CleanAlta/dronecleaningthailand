FAQ

What does CleanAlta Thailand clean with drones?

We focus on facades, glass, roofs, cladding, hard-to-access structures and selected exterior surfaces where a drone-based method is operationally relevant.

Can you work on occupied sites?

Yes. Missions are prepared around safety perimeters, timing constraints and site coordination in order to protect occupants, guests and ongoing operations.
