Drone Cleaning for Hotels & Resorts in Thailand

Hotels operate under constant visibility pressure. Drone cleaning delivers premium exterior results with zero guest disruption.

Why hotels choose drone cleaning

Scaffolding creates visual disruption near guest rooms, blocks pool areas and entrances, generates noise complaints and takes weeks to install. Drone cleaning eliminates all of these issues — deploying same-day, operating during off-peak hours and leaving zero visual footprint.

Thailand's hotel market opportunity

Thailand has 17,126 hotels with 711,729 rooms. An estimated 600-800 are 4-5 star properties concentrated in Bangkok (250-350), Phuket (150-200), Pattaya (80-120) and Koh Samui (40-60). Major groups include Minor Hotels (Anantara/Avani), Centara, Dusit, Accor and Marriott. Hotel facility budgets have increased 10-15% recently.

ROI for hotel engineering teams

A typical 200-room beachfront resort spends 800K-1.5M THB annually on exterior cleaning. Drone cleaning reduces this to 450K-900K THB while increasing cleaning frequency from 2x to 4x per year. The result: cleaner facades more often, at lower total cost.
