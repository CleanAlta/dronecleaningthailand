About CleanAlta Thailand

Professional drone cleaning for facades, glass, roofs and structures. Part of the CleanAlta Group with 10+ years of drone operations experience.

Our story

CleanAlta was founded on a simple observation: traditional building cleaning methods — scaffolding, rope access, gondola systems — are expensive, slow, dangerous and disruptive. Drone technology offers a fundamentally better approach that is safer, faster, cheaper and produces superior results with comprehensive documentation.

Why Thailand

Thailand's combination of 17,000+ hotels, 3,000+ high-rise buildings in Bangkok alone, aggressive tropical weathering and a growing facility management market creates ideal conditions for drone cleaning adoption. The market is virtually uncontested — no dedicated drone cleaning operator currently serves Thailand.

Our approach

We don't just clean buildings. We deliver a corporate-grade service with SLA, SOP, method statements, safety plans, HD before/after documentation and engineer-reviewed reports. Every mission is CAAT compliant, fully insured, and executed by certified pilots with 10+ years of commercial experience.

CleanAlta Group

CleanAlta Thailand is part of the CleanAlta Group, headquartered in France with operations in Switzerland. The group brings 10+ years of professional drone operations experience, proprietary cleaning methods and an international network of partners.
