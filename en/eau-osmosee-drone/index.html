Why this service is specified this way

Why pure water matters

Pure water helps reduce mineral residue and supports cleaner drying on surfaces where finish quality is critical.

Typical applications

Used for glass, smooth cladding, solar panels and assets where marks and streaks are operationally or visually undesirable.

How CleanAlta applies it

The method is combined with the right flight path, rinse logic, site control and post-execution verification.

FAQ

Can this service be delivered on occupied sites?

Yes. Site occupation, guest circulation, residents and technical constraints are built into the mission preparation process.

How do you decide between pure water, low pressure and high pressure?

The choice is made after reviewing the material, contamination profile, finish expectations and operational risk.
