CleanAlta Thailand

Compliance & Certifications

Every CleanAlta mission meets CAAT, NBTC and Thai insurance requirements.

🛡️

CAAT Operator Certificate

Registered via UAS Portal (uasportal.caat.or.th). All drones registered with Civil Aviation Authority of Thailand. Certificate renewed annually.

📡

NBTC Registration

All drone radio equipment registered with National Broadcasting and Telecommunications Commission. Compliance verified for each unit.

💳

THB 2,000,000 Insurance

Third-party liability coverage exceeding CAAT minimum of 1M THB. Covers all drone operations including close-proximity building work.

🎓

CAAT Pilot Certificates

All operators passed CAAT 40-question exam (75% threshold). Drone aviation certificates maintained current. 10+ years commercial experience.

Flight authorization process

Commercial drone cleaning in Bangkok and other controlled airspace requires pre-mission CAAT flight plan submission via the UAS Portal, minimum 3 days in advance. CleanAlta handles all regulatory submissions as part of every project — clients do not need to manage any aviation paperwork.

Safety standards

Every mission includes: ground-level safety perimeters, site coordination with building management, real-time communication between pilot and ground crew, pre-flight equipment checks, and post-mission documentation. Emergency procedures are briefed before each operation.

Insurance and liability

Our operations are covered by comprehensive third-party liability insurance of THB 2,000,000 per flight — double the CAAT minimum requirement. This covers property damage and personal injury arising from drone operations.
