Low-Pressure Drone Cleaning in Thailand

Controlled, gentle cleaning for heritage buildings, temples and architectural surfaces that require preservation over force.

Preservation-first approach

Heritage assets, temples, historical buildings and properties with delicate surface finishes demand cleaning methods that prioritize preservation. Our low-pressure drone systems operate at controlled pressures that remove soiling without risking surface damage, erosion or finish degradation.

Thailand heritage applications

Thailand's rich architectural heritage includes thousands of temples (wat), colonial-era buildings, traditional shophouses and protected structures. Many are located in Chiang Mai's Old City, Bangkok's Rattanakosin Island, and historic districts across the country. Low-pressure drone cleaning provides an alternative to manual cleaning that is safer for both the structure and the workers.
