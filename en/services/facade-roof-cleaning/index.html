CleanAlta Thailand

Drone Facade & Roof Cleaning in Thailand

Eliminate scaffolding. Clean any facade or roof with drone-based precision adapted to each surface material.

When this service is relevant

Suitable for facades, cladding, roofs and building envelopes affected by pollution, humidity, biological growth or visual deterioration. Particularly effective on buildings over 4 stories where scaffolding costs become significant — hotels, condominiums, office towers and industrial facilities across Bangkok, Phuket, Koh Samui and Chiang Mai.

How the method is selected

Our team selects the optimal combination of pure water (heated to 70°C for enhanced cleaning), low-pressure for delicate materials, or high-pressure for robust surfaces. The decision is based on a pre-mission site survey evaluating material type, soiling profile, age of surface and finish requirements.

Coverage and performance

Our drone systems clean 1,000-1,500 m² per hour — 10-15x faster than manual scaffolding-based methods. A typical 20-story hotel facade in Bangkok can be completed in 1-3 days versus 2-6 weeks with traditional methods.

What the client receives

Every project includes: method statement, safety perimeter plan, CAAT flight authorization documentation, HD before/after visuals, engineer-reviewed mission report, and maintenance schedule recommendations. All packaged in a corporate Proof Pack designed for asset managers, juristic offices and ownership teams.

Relevant markets

This service is available across all CleanAlta Thailand priority markets: Bangkok, Phuket, Koh Samui, Krabi and Chiang Mai.
