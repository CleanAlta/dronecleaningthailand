Drone Building Inspection in Thailand

Combine cleaning with HD visual and thermal inspection. Detect cracks, water ingress, thermal leaks in a single mission.

Cleaning + inspection in one mission

During every cleaning mission, our drones capture high-resolution imagery of the entire building envelope. This visual data is reviewed by structural engineers to identify maintenance issues before they become costly repairs: cracks, sealant failures, water ingress points, thermal anomalies, corrosion and surface degradation.

Thermal imaging capability

Infrared cameras mounted alongside cleaning systems detect thermal anomalies invisible to the naked eye: heat leaks through window seals, moisture trapped behind cladding, electrical hotspots, and insulation failures. This is particularly valuable for energy-efficiency audits and preventive maintenance programs.

Engineer-reviewed reports

All inspection data is compiled into structured reports reviewed by qualified engineers. Reports include annotated images, severity ratings, recommended remediation actions and priority timelines. Delivered via the Proof Pack system within 48 hours of mission completion.
