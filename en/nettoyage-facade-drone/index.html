Why this service is specified this way

When this service is relevant

Suitable for facades, cladding, roofs and exterior building envelopes affected by pollution, humidity, biological growth or visual deterioration.

How the method is selected

The operating team chooses the right combination of pure water, low pressure or higher-pressure cleaning according to the support, finish level and soiling profile.

What the client receives

A corporate-ready package with mission preparation, protected work zones, before/after visuals and a Proof Pack for decision-makers.

FAQ

Can this service be delivered on occupied sites?

Yes. Site occupation, guest circulation, residents and technical constraints are built into the mission preparation process.

How do you decide between pure water, low pressure and high pressure?

The choice is made after reviewing the material, contamination profile, finish expectations and operational risk.
