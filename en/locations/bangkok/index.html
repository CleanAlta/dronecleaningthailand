CleanAlta Thailand

Drone Cleaning Services in Bangkok

Bangkok has 3,000+ high-rise buildings requiring regular exterior maintenance. CleanAlta delivers drone cleaning for towers, hotels and condominiums.

Bangkok market profile

Bangkok ranks 13th globally for total skyscrapers. The city has over 3,000 buildings above 8 stories, including premium condominiums, 5-star hotels, Grade A office towers and mixed-use developments. Over 60% of existing office supply is buildings over 20 years old — creating substantial facade maintenance demand.

Key areas we serve

Sukhumvit corridor: Luxury hotels, premium condominiums (Thonglor, Asoke, Phrom Phong). Silom/Sathorn: Office towers, international hotels. Riverside: Major hotel brands (Mandarin Oriental, Peninsula, Shangri-La). Rama 9/New CBD: Modern office towers and mixed-use. Ploenchit/Wireless: Embassy district, premium commercial.

Bangkok-specific considerations

Operations within the Bangkok VTR1 Restricted Zone (19 km radius from Victory Monument) require explicit CAAT authorization, which CleanAlta handles as part of every project. The dense urban environment requires careful flight planning, safety perimeters and coordination with building management.
