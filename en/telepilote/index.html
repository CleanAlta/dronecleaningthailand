Who is TELEPILOTE SAS?

TELEPILOTE SAS is the leading European drone training center and civil drone pioneer. Founded in 2014, the company has trained thousands of professional drone pilots and operates under full EASA (European Union Aviation Safety Agency) certification. TELEPILOTE is the parent company of CleanAlta, providing the technical expertise, pilot certification and regulatory knowledge that powers every CleanAlta cleaning mission.
